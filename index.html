Welcome to Hackton Manor!

In the small village of Hackville, lies an abandoned mansion shrouded in fear and mystery. Legends talk of an angry spirit haunting its halls. One day, as you are out exploring the village, you stumble upon Hackton Manor, awakening the vengeful ghost inside. Now a terrifying chase ensures through the building as you try to escape its clutches...

Game Rules

  1. 1. Escape the mansion by answering questions and completing puzzles.
  2. 2. Enter doors and complete the task successfully to progress.
  3. 3. Score 1-3 points depending on how difficult the room is.
  4. 4. The difficulty level is determined by how damaged the door is.
  5. 5. Look out for the special ? doors containing puzzles.
  6. 6. If you complete your door task, you will progress to a new set of doors.
  7. 7. If you fail, the door will be barred shut and the ghost will get closer.
  8. 8. Collect thirty points to escape!
