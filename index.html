Game Rules

  1. 1. You must escape the mansion by answering questions correctly.
  2. 2. There are three doors to choose from with increasing difficulties.
  3. 3. Score one - three points dependant on your difficulty. Easy, Medium and Hard.
  4. 4. Answer the question wrong and the ghost gains the points.
  5. 5. You begin four steps ahead of the ghost.
  6. 6. Answer an easy question wrong, the ghost moves one step closer.
  7. 7. Answer a medium question wrong, the ghost moves two steps closer.
  8. 8. Answer a hard question wrong, the ghost moves three steps closer.
  9. 9. Collect thirty points to escape!
  10. 10. Do you have what it takes to escape? Begin the game below!
